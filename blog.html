MY WORK EXPERIENCE AS A JANITOR
By: Benjamin R. Fancubit
Date: August 3, 2025
In this post of the day, Benjamin represents his job experiences as a student to inspire and encourage others who are working while studying.

Introduction

Balancing school and work has always been a challenge, but it has also shaped me into a more responsible and focused individual. My work experience as a janitor has taught me valuable life lessons that no classroom could ever fully provide.

My Work Experience

As a janitor at the University of the Cordilleras, I began my journey by handling basic cleaning duties — sweeping floors, emptying trash bins, and sanitizing rooms. I arrived early every day to make sure that the campus was a clean and safe environment for students and staff alike.

I took pride in what I did, even when it was physically exhausting. While mopping floors or dusting desks, I found quiet moments of reflection. Each task reminded me of the importance of humility, discipline, and perseverance.

There were days when I felt tired or overwhelmed — balancing exams, lectures, and an 8-hour shift wasn't easy. But through it all, I never lost sight of my goals. My job gave me perspective, built my character, and helped me value every peso I earned.

Conclusion

Working as a janitor while pursuing my degree in Criminology is not something I’m ashamed of — in fact, I’m proud of it. It taught me resilience, gave me purpose, and built my work ethic. I learned how to manage time, communicate with others, and take responsibility. I hope my story reminds other students that no job is too small and every effort counts. If you are working and studying, know this: you are strong, and your dedication will pay off.

Keep pushing forward, even if it feels tough. Because every step you take, no matter how hard, is a step closer to your dreams.
